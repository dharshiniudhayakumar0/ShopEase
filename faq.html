Frequently Asked Questions

What is your shipping policy?
We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $75. For orders below $75, a flat shipping fee of $15 applies. Most orders are processed within 1-2 business days and delivered within 3-5 business days.
How can I return or exchange a product?
We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. The product must be unused and in its original packaging. To initiate a return, please visit your account page or contact our support team.
Are your products cruelty-free?
Absolutely! We are committed to ethical beauty. All products sold on ShopEase are 100% cruelty-free and have never been tested on animals.
Do you ship internationally?
Currently, we ship within the United States and Canada. We are working diligently to expand our shipping services to more countries in the near future.
How do I track my order?
Once your order has shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to the carrier's website. You can also track your order directly from your profile dashboard under "Recent Orders".
